Corporate Sponsorship

Standing Room Only relies on the generosity and support of our local business community to provide the resources and opportunities that make our theatre program shine. Your contribution directly benefits the talented students at Highlands High School and Highlands Middle School.

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, all donations are tax-deductible, and we are happy to provide a receipt for your records. By supporting our program, you are not just helping us create memorable shows—you are investing in the arts and the future of our young performers. Thank you for considering a partnership with us in making our high school theatre program a success!
